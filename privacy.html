Policies for On-line Visitors Under 13 Years of Age

When it comes to the collection of personal information from children under the age of 13 years old, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) puts parents in control. The Federal Trade Commission, United States' consumer protection agency, enforces the COPPA Rule, which spells out what operators of websites and online services must do to protect children's privacy and safety online. We do not specifically market to children under the age of 13 years old and do not intend to collect personal information from children under age 13 without permission of the child's parent or legal guardian. Users under 13 years of age should not use Autobeat Player and submit personal information to us without the explicit permission from their parent or legal guardian. If your child has submitted personal information and you would like to request that such information be removed from our system, please contact us via email.
